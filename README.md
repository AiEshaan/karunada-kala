# Karunada Kala (ಕರ್ನಾಟಕದ ಕಲೆ) 🏺
### AI-Powered Cultural Ecosystem Platform

**Karunada Kala** is a world-class cultural discovery and preservation platform built as a tribute to the rich heritage of Karnataka. Developed using **Jetpack Compose** and **Google Gemini AI**, it serves as a "Directory of Pride," connecting modern audiences with traditional artisans, workshops, and performances.

## 🌟 Vision & Impact
In a globalized world, traditional art forms like **Yakshagana**, **Kinnala toys**, and **Bidriware** often struggle for visibility. Karunada Kala bridges this gap by:
- **Cultural Sovereignty**: Preserving Karnataka's unique identity through high-fidelity digital archiving.
- **Creative Economy**: Providing direct market access and "Guru-Shishya" mentorship opportunities for rural artisans.
- **Cultural Tourism**: Mapping authentic workshops and performances to encourage heritage-based travel.

## 🛠 Features
- **Art Form Explorer**: A curated catalog with parallax headers and cinematic animations.
- **Artisan Map**: A custom "Parchment-style" map locating authentic makers and live events.
- **Workshop Sign-up**: A seamless registration flow for "Guru-Shishya" learning programs.
- **Artisan Profiles**: Full profiles featuring "Tap to Call" and WhatsApp integration.
- **My Journey**: A gamified dashboard tracking your cultural engagements with unlockable badges.

## 🧠 GenAI Integration
- **Kala AI Assistant**: A specialized heritage chatbot powered by **Gemini 1.5 Pro**.
- **3D Legend Flip**: Interactive cards that reveal personalized legends generated by Gemini.
- **Real-time Translation**: Instant translation of cultural narratives into Kannada, Hindi, and English.

## 🏗 Tech Stack
- **UI**: Jetpack Compose (Material 3)
- **Backend**: Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Storage)
- **AI**: Google Gemini Pro (Vertex AI / AI Studio)
- **Maps**: Google Maps Compose SDK
- **Architecture**: MVVM with Clean Architecture

## 🚀 How to Run
1. **Clone the repository**:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/AiEshaan/karunada-kala.git
   ```
2. **Open in Android Studio**: Import the project and wait for Gradle sync.
3. **API Keys**:
   - Add your `MAPS_API_KEY` in `local.properties`.
   - Add your `GEMINI_API_KEY` in `local.properties`.
4. **Build and Run**: Connect an Android device/emulator and press **Run**.
